Barbell Bicep Curl

The barbell bicep curl is a fundamental exercise for building arm strength and size by targeting the biceps. To perform the lift:

  • Stand upright with your feet shoulder-width apart, holding a barbell with an underhand grip.
  • Keep your elbows close to your torso and your back straight.
  • Slowly curl the barbell upward by contracting your biceps until it reaches shoulder level.
  • Pause briefly at the top, then lower the barbell back to the starting position in a controlled manner.
