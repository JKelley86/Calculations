Barbell Floor Press

The barbell floor press is a compound exercise that targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps. It is an excellent alternative to the traditional bench press for building upper body strength. To perform the lift:

  • Lie flat on the floor with your knees bent and feet flat on the ground.
  • Grip the barbell slightly wider than shoulder-width and hold it above your chest with your arms fully extended.
  • Slowly lower the barbell until your upper arms touch the floor, keeping your elbows at a 45-degree angle.
  • Pause briefly, then press the barbell back up to the starting position.
