Barbell Back Squat

The barbell back squat is a compound exercise that primarily targets the quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, and lower back. To perform the lift:

  • Start with the barbell resting on your upper traps, feet shoulder-width apart.
  • Keep your chest up, back straight, and core engaged.
  • Lower your body by bending at the hips and knees until your thighs are at least parallel to the ground.
  • Push through your heels to return to the starting position.
