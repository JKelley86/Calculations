Buffalo Chicken Dip

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 20 minutes

Total Time: 35 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups shredded cooked rotisserie chicken
  • 2 (8 Ounch) packages of cream cheese (softened)
  • 3/4 Cups hot sauce
  • 1/2-1 Cup shredded pepper jack cheese

Instructions

  1. Turn on crock pot to high.
  2. Place Cream Cheese in crock pot.
  3. Shred Chicken and put in crock pot.
  4. Place shredded Pepper Jack Cheese in crock pot.
  5. Pour Franks Red Hot in crock pot and Mix.
  6. Let sit until cheese is completely melted.
