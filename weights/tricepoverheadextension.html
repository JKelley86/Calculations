Tricep Overhead Extension

The tricep overhead extension is an excellent exercise to target the triceps and improve arm strength. To perform the lift:

  • Hold a dumbbell or barbell with both hands and lift it overhead, keeping your arms fully extended.
  • Keep your elbows close to your head and your core engaged to maintain balance.
  • Slowly lower the weight behind your head by bending your elbows while keeping your upper arms stationary.
  • Pause briefly at the bottom, then extend your arms to return to the starting position.
