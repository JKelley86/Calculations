Banana Cream Cool Whip Bars

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 30

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of flour
  • 4 Tablespoons butter
  • 1 Large container of cool whip
  • 1 Package cream cheese
  • 1 Cup powdered sugar
  • 2 Boxes of banana cream instant pudding
  • 2 1/2 Cups of milk
  • 1 Banana sliced

Instructions

  1. Combine flower and butter in small bowl.
  2. Press mixture into the bottom of a cake pan and bake for 15 minutes or until slightly golden... let cool.
  3. In a bowl combine banana cream pudding and milk.
  4. Mix with a wire whisk until it thickens.
  5. Put the cream cheese on top of the crust.
  6. Put banana cream pudding on top of the cream cheese.
  7. Then put a layer of cool whip on top of the banana cream pudding
  8. Top it off with banana slices
