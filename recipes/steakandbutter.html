Steak And Butter

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Total Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 2 Heads of garlic
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. For the butter Soften 2 cups of butter and chop 1 whole garlic Head.
  2. Mix together in a bowl with thyme (Fresh or powder).
  3. Set in fridge to harden.
  4. For the steaks heat your grill to 400°F once it’s at temp put your cast iron pan on the grill with some butter and a couple cloves of garlic.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes or until golden brown.Then unpack your steaks and pat dry with a paper towel season generously with salt and pepper on both sides and pat seasoning into steaks.Allow to cool before serving.
  6. Cook each side for roughly 3-4 minutes or until internal temp reaches 130°F set on a plate or tray and cut thin slices
  7. Pour butter over steak and serve.
