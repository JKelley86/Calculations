Mozzarella Sticks

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 20 minutes (45 minute wait time)

Cook Time: 8 minutes

Total Time: 28 minutes

Ingredients

  • 12 Pack of string cheese
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 Cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 Cups italian breadcrumbs
  • 1 Cup all prupose flour

Instructions

  1. Unwrap string cheese and place on freezer safe dish.
  2. Freeze for 45 minutes and no longer.
  3. Using 3 medium sized bowls, beat Eggs in 1 bowl, combine Parmesan Cheese and Breadcrumbs in the second bowl, then place Flour in the third bowl..
  4. Remove cheese sticks from freezer and dip each on into the flour, then eggs, then breadcrumbs.
  5. Lightly oil the bottom of the air fryer or line with parchment paper.
  6. Air fry mozzarella sticks for 3 minutes at 400Â°F.
  7. Rotate the mozzarella sticks and continue cooking for an additional 5 minutes or until golden brown.
