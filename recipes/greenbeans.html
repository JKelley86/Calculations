Green Beans

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 20 minutes

Total Time: 40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 Head of garlic
  • Green beans
  • Soy sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Ginger
  • Red pepper flakes

Instructions

  1. Cut the ends of the green beans and boil until pliable.
  2. Dry the green beans off and fry until slightly brown.
  3. Add olive oil to pan and cook a little longer.
  4. Add garlic, ginger, red pepper flakes, and soy sauce.
  5. Fry an additional 5-10 minutes and serve.
