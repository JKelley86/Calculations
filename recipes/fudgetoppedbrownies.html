Fudge Topped Brownies

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 40 minutes

Total Time: 55 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of butter
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Cup of sugar
  • 1/2 Cup of milk
  • 1 1/2 Teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 2/3 Cup hershey's cocoa
  • 1/2 Teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 Cup flour
  • 12oz Semi sweet chocolate chips
  • 14oz Sweetened condensed milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Place holder instructions for brownies.
  3. Pour brownie batter in 9X13 pan and bake at 350 for 40 minutes.
  4. Mix chocolate chips, vanilla extract, and condensed milk in bowl.
  5. Pour mixture over brownies, cool and chill, then serve.
  6. Store in room temprature.
