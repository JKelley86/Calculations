Welcome To The Filters page

This page will tell you everything you need to know about the filters in your house.

Filters

Furnace Filter: Should be replaced every other month with the next date being Jan 1st 2025. Your air filter size is 16"X25"X1".

Air Exchanger Filter: This filter should be cleaned out every 3 months with the next date being Jan 1st 2025.

Fridge Water Filter: The fridge will tell you when to replace it. You will need to run 5 gallons of water through the water dispenser after changing.
