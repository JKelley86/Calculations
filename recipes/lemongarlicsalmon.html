Lemon Garlic Salmon

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 Skin on salmon filets
  • 4 Tablespoons of butter
  • 1 Head of garlic minced
  • 1 Teaspoon salt
  • 1 Teaspoon fresh dill chopped
  • 1 Tablespoon fresh parsley chopped
  • Juice of 1 lemon

Instructions

  1. Melt butter in bowl and mix in all seasonings.
  2. Coat the top of the salmon filets with butter mixture.
  3. Line the bottom of the air fryer basket with parchment paper.
  4. Air fry at 350Â°F for 8 minutes.
  5. Flip filets and coat with remaining butter sauce.
  6. Continue cooking for another 7 minutes.
