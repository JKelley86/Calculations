Blooming Onion

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 15 minutes (2 hour wait time)

Cook Time: 22 minutes

Total Time: 37 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 Large sweet onion
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 Cup of milk
  • 2 1/2 Cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 Teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 Teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 Teaspoon pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F.
  2. Slice 1-Inch off top of Onion so it will stand do not slice off the root end. Place the cut side down on a cutting board.
  3. Beginning about 1/2 inch down from the root make 12 slices down through the onion.
  4. Fully submerge onion in a bowl of ice water and let soak for 2 hours. Remove onion form ice water and pat dry with towel.
  5. In a medium sized bowl, beat together Eggs and Milk. In a separate medium sized bowl, mix dry ingredients.
  6. Pour Egg wash over Onion with ladle to coat outside and inside crevices well. Then sprinkle Onion generously with dry ingredients.
  7. Repeat step 6
  8. Lightly oil or line the bottom of the air fryer basket with parchment paper. Place the Onion inside the air fryer and fry at 400° F for 22 minutes
