French Fries

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Unpeeled potatoes
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Leaving the potatoes unpeeled cut them in half length wise then cut each half into halves also length wise.
  2. Then cut into desired strips for the fries.
  3. Once there all cut wash them thoroughly. Once washed pat dry.
  4. Put the dried fries in a bowl and drizzle olive oil over them and mix it around. Add salt and mix
  5. Air fryer at 380Â° after 14-16 minutes check them. If there not done shake the basket and continue air frying.
