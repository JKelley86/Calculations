Apple Salad

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 0 minutes

Total Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 Large Cool Whip
  • 1/2 Package of dry vanilla pudding mix
  • 4 Granny smith apples
  • 5 or 6 Large Snicker candy bars

Instructions

  1. Mix cool whip and pudding mix in a large bowl.
  2. Cut up apples and snickers.
  3. Add apples and snickers to bowl and mix.
