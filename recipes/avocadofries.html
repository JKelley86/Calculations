Avocado Fries

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 Avocados (Not fully ripe)
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/4 Cup of flower
  • 1/2 Cup of panko breadcrumbs

Instructions

  1. Cut avocados in half lengthwise. Then cut into slices and remove from skin. Drizzle with lime juice then salt and pepper the slices.
  2. Using 3 small bowls, beat eggs in bowl 1, add flower to the second bowl, then place breadcrumbs in third bowl.
  3. Dip avocado slices in the egg wash, then coat with flower then coat in breadcrumbs.
  4. Place avocado slices in the air fryer and fry at 400Â° F for 15 minutes or until crispy.
