Biscuits And Gravy

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 30

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound of sausage
  • 1 Can of biscuits
  • 2 Tablespoons of butter
  • 2 Tablespoons of flour
  • 1/4 Teaspoon of salt (Optinal)
  • 1 Teaspoon of coarse ground pepper
  • 2 1/4 Cups of milk

Instructions

  1. Prepare biscuits according to the package.
  2. Cook sausage in pan.
  3. In same pan melt butter over medium heat.
  4. Add flour, salt, and pepper and whisk. When mixture bubbles reduce heat to low and cook for additional 1-2 minutes.
  5. Gradually mix in milk until smooth.
  6. Bring gravy to boil over medium heat stirring often. Cook for additional 1-2 minutes or until it reaches desired consistancy.
  7. Split biscuits in half and drizzle gravy on top
