Chicken Mozzarella Sticks

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound ground chicken
  • 1 Block mozzarella cheese (1lb)
  • 1 6oz Bag shredded parmesan
  • Pork rine crumbs
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Italian seasoning
  • Marinara sauce

Instructions

  1. Mix parmesan cheese and pork rines on a plate (This will be used as coating so the whole bag will not be necessary).
  2. Mix seasoning's with ground chicken (Season to your own liking).
  3. Cut mozzarella block into sticks.
  4. Cover the mozzarella sticks with ground chicken.
  5. Roll the mozzarella sticks in the bread crumb mixture.
  6. Place mozzarella sticks in the airfryer at 390Â°F for 15 minutes.
