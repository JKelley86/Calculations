Barbell Bench Press

The barbell bench press is a compound exercise that primarily targets the chest, triceps, and shoulders. To perform the lift:

  • Lie on a flat bench with your feet firmly planted on the ground.
  • Grip the barbell slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.
  • Lower the barbell slowly to your chest, keeping your elbows at a 45-degree angle.
  • Press the barbell back up to the starting position until your arms are fully extended.
