Sweet Potato Fries

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Total Time: 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 Sweet potatoes
  • 1 Tablespoon olive or avacado oil
  • 1/2 Teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Peel and slice potatoes into french fry sized slices.
  2. Place into a large bowl and coat with oil and salt.
  3. Place fries into air fryer basket forming an even layer.
  4. Cook at 400Â°F for 5 minutes. Then shake the air fryer basket to mix fries.
  5. Continue air frying for an additional 15 minutes continuing to shake up fries every 5 minutes until done.
  6. Cooking times may vary depending on the size of potato slices.
